Never existence true love

Once upon a time, Alex, 18 years old man who is very talented, handsome, tall, did a well job when he was in high school, and applied to a good university also. His life sounds very successful, however, he has never had a girlfriend before.

As usual, he went out with his friends, they went to play basketball, played ice hockey, and had a wonderful party at the midnight. He is happy, however, there was something miss in his heart.

After the party, he walked back to home lonely just like before, the weather is cold, the sigh is dark, the environment is very quiet, just like his heart. At the same time, he was thinking about why he is so talented but non of the girls like him.

He went back home, he said loudly “I am back!!”, he knows no one is there, however, he still wishes that there is a sound can give him a response. No one talk to him, he did everything by himself, then go to bed. On the bed, he is still thinking about when can he find his true love.

One week after, he went to a bar with his friend, just like before, and he saw a girl who does not have a special appearance, but give him a special feeling. He went to chat with the girl, the girl is intelligent and had a great time chat with Alex. Alex is waiting for 18 years and he finally find a girl who gave him a strong feeling.

He invited the girl to have a dinner with him, she agreed. This was the first time he went out with a girl without any friends. They went to watch a movie, see the night view of the city, the atmosphere was romantic, Alex fell in love with the girl.

One day, Alex saw the girl went out with a man, his heart is broken, he wants to know who is the boy. By asking his friend, he realized that the girl has already had someone who she likes, but the person is not him.

He cannot believe the truth, and he is still thinking about she can be his girlfriend in the future. He tried to memorize the screen when they were having fun together. “impossible” he said, but the truth is showing in front of him. The girl does not like him.

He is very said, he starts to think about the interaction between him and the girl, they really had a great time, he also thinks that the girl likes him, just when he was thinking, the girl message him “I have a boyfriend”. His heart is broken.

The girl felt in love with her boy friend quickly, Alex can see they went out happily, and his heart is bleeding, the girl always posts some photos that they went out on facebook, he is jealous, the photos are like bullet, shot on his heart mercilessly.

The girl gives him a lesson, never believe true love, the true love is never existent in the world, no matter what, the girl may just have a friend feeling on you, the desire of love in his heart is gone, he can live alone by himself, just like before, he does not need girlfriend because he believes that the love is never exist.
